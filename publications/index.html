publications

My research spans the theory and practice of machine learning, network and data science, and optimization. I often find it helpful to browse, ask, and answer research-related questions on cstheory.stackexchange.com, a theoretical computer science Q&A site where I am also a moderator.

My papers are listed below in reverse chronological order by year. Note that authors on all of my publications appear alphabetically. Citations to my papers can be found on Google Scholar. Paper tags are colored as follows: journal article conference article editorial work manuscript .
